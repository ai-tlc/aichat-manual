## 3.1 Further configuration {#3-1-configuration}


Open **Settings** (person icon, bottom left). Choose theme (light/dark). Under **Personalization**:


- **Memory Creation:** Allow the AI to save info from prior prompts/conversations (e.g., your field, hobbies).
- **Memory Context:** Enable using stored memories in new chats.
- **Memory Management:** Review, adjust, or delete saved memories.
- **Custom Instructions:** Define default behavior/style.


**Example – Custom Instructions**


> “Always answer in Dutch. Write as an academic advisor: supportive, critical, and focused on improving my work. Use formal language and avoid excessive jargon. Structure complex answers with bullet points.”

## 3.2 Customized interaction: working with personas {#3-2-personas}


**Personas** are predefined roles that shape the AI’s **tone, expertise, and behavior**, ideal for recurring tasks and consistent outputs.


**Standard personas** include:


- **Course Manual Creator** – collaborates on course manuals.
- **Socratic Coach** – supports understanding via questions.
- **Technical Assistant** – explains UvA AI Chat features and solves issues.
- **Language Assistant** – grammar, spelling, and style improvements.


**Create your own persona**


1. **Title** (short name).
2. **Description** (role/purpose; non‑behavioral).
3. **Model** (e.g., GPT‑4o).
4. **Conversation style** (Balanced, Creative, Precise).
5. **Behavior description** (detailed tone/expertise/actions).
6. **Upload files** (manuals/style guides).
7. **Save**.


**Example – “Critical Research Assistant”**
Tone: constructive yet critical; structure feedback as (1) brief summary, (2) strengths, (3) detailed improvements with examples and probing questions.

## 3.3 “Groups”: collaborating and sharing with others {#3-3-groups}


Create **Groups** (two‑person icon) to share files, prompts, personas, and chats.


**Create a group**


- **Name, Description**
- **Members** (emails) and **Owners** (emails; can edit)
- **Personas / Prompts** (optional)
- **Start / End Dates** (optional)


Teachers might add students as **Members** to use a specific persona; **Owners** can edit.


---


## 3.4 “My Projects”: your organized workspace {#3-4-projects}


Under **My Projects** (folder icon), organize chats, prompts, and personas per project. Click **+ Add Project**. Keep multiple topic‑related conversations together and return to earlier prompts/results quickly.
